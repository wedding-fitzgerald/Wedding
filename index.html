Our Story

Matthew and Emma met in 2018 at Lagoon Amusement Park. At the time Matt was in charge of training Emma to be the best Games Attendant in the department. Little did they know that someday they would be getting married. Two years later the couple became supervisors, they didn’t interact much their first year as supervisors. However, their second year as supervisors Emma was given Matthew’s number by a friend and they began to talk on a daily basis. Shortly after they started texting, they started hanging out outside of work. Then on Emma’s 18th birthday the couple had their first kiss at the drive-in movie theater that has since been closed. The next day Matthew grew a pair and asked Emma to be his girlfriend. The rest is history, the couple enjoy going to Dave and Busters, the aquarium, fishing, and the couch to cuddle, play Minecraft and cuddle with Midnight. They got engaged on April Fools Day of 2023 on a cruise to Mexico. Emma and Matthew have been through a lot together and are excited to spend the rest of their lives together.
